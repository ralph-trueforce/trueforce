Contact Center Consulting

Trueforce is your trusted partner for cloud contact center services. Our track record of more than 15 years shows that we understand every business has unique needs for its contact centers. We are proven experts, both technical and non-technical, who work within all layers of your organization to ensure easy implementation and confidence at all levels of your workforce. We have deep experience integrating solutions from industry leaders. Join forces with us and gain access to an intelligent platform of services that contribute to your business growth.

Implementation Process

Our team's mission is to understand your needs and your workflow and create a customized solution. We hold sessions with key stakeholders and perform in-depth analysis of current, past and future processes to drill down to the most effective solution for your business.

Here's an overview of our contact center implementation process:


1. KICKOFF
Our project team meets yours and establishes a project timeline

2. DISCOVER
We hold discovery sessions with key stakeholders to conduct a thorough review of all processes

3. ANALYZE
We conduct a detailed analysis of business processes, current and future, with an eye toward improvement


4. DESIGN
We create a full, written design of our solutions, including features, customization and data integration

5. BUILD
We create, install and test the customized contact center solution for your business

6. LAUNCH
We deliver comprehensive training on how to use the new solution and facilitate employee, supervisor and end-user adoption
